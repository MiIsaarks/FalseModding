# Forgotten Skill Tweaks

A gameplay tweak mod for Risk of Rain 2 that rebalances and slightly reworks the less used skills.

---

##  False Son Tweaks
* **Laser of The Father:** Laser base charge duration reduced to 1s, Laser fire frequency now correctly scales with attack speed, proc coefficient per tick increased to 0.7, removed damage fall off, Growth now increases the damage by 5% instead of increasing the duration, increased the laser damage by 35%, refills 50% of the spikes.
* **Brother's Lightning:** Every 10 hits with the laser summons a lightning strike dealing 300% of the laser's damage to the target.

##  Huntress Tweaks
* **Arrow Rain:** Increased area . Cooldown adjusted to 11s. Increased damage per second to 660%, increased proc coefficient to 0.7 . increased tick frequency and now scales with attack speed.
* **Blink:** Cooldown reduced to 4.5s.

##  Artificer Tweaks
* **Flamethrower:** Greatly increased range. Adds a small jump on cast, tick damage increased by 2.5x, now has 100% ignite chance.

---

##  Installation
1. Install [BepInExPack](https://thunderstore.io/package/bbepis/BepInExPack/) and [R2API-Language](https://thunderstore.io/package/RiskofThunder/R2API_Language/).
2. Extract `ForgottenSkillsTweaks.dll` into your `BepInEx/plugins/` folder (or install directly via r2modman / Thunderstore Mod Manager).
